- repository: https://github.com/gwoltman/gpuowl
- commit: `294cc485ac8cf53c8b69144a3039832eda573849`
- original GPUOwl repository: https://github.com/preda/gpuowl
- original author: Mihai Preda
- later GPUOwl/PRPLL and NTT work: George Woltman
- upstream project names: GPUOwl and PRPLL

Register-engine design reference:

- project: Marin
- repository: https://github.com/galloty/marin
- author: Yves Gallot
- license: MIT

Aevum does not claim to be an official release of GPUOwl, PRPLL or Marin. The original GPUOwl/PRPLL README is preserved in `README_GPUOWL.md`.
